Our Mission

Finding affordable, relatable mentorship as a student is incredibly tough. Traditional tutoring centers can charge up to 1 million VND per session, and private professional teachers typically ask for 750k per session—prices that many students simply can't afford. But even more than the cost, there’s the disconnect: these tutors often don’t fully grasp the unique pressures and challenges that come with studying your specific curriculum, your subjects, or the mental stress you face.

That’s where we come in. We’re building a community where students help students. Here, you can connect with mentors who are studying the same subjects, following the same curriculum, and who genuinely understand what you’re going through—because they’re in the same boat. And the best part? Sessions can be as affordable as 350k VND.

We believe that everyone has something valuable to share, and everyone should have the chance to benefit from it. Whether you’re looking for guidance or have knowledge to offer, we’re here to help you connect and grow—without breaking the bank.
